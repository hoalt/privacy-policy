Delete collected information on Facebook

1. Go to "Settings and Privacy" in your Facebook account, then click "Settings".

2. Search for "Apps and Websites" and you will see all the apps and websites associated with your Facebook account.

3. Search and click "Name of the application you want to remove information" on the search bar.

4. Highlight the options according to your needs and click the "Delete" button.

5. Congratulations! You have successfully deleted your app activities
